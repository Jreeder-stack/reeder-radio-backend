GENERAL TOLERANCE RULES:
- Matching must be case-insensitive
- Ignore filler words (uh, um, ah, standby)
- Ignore leading or trailing words
- Ignore punctuation
- Ignore pluralization
- Ignore minor word breaks or merges
- First valid status match wins
- If confidence is low or ambiguous, remain silent
