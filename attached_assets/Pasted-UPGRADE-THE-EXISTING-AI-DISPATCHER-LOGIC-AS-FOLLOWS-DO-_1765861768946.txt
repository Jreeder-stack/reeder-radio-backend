- The AI dispatcher call sign is "Central"

"Central, <UnitID>"
"Central <UnitID>"

"<UnitID>, go ahead."

- No other responses allowed

STATE B: AWAITING STATUS

- If no valid status is received within 15 seconds, reset to IDLE silently

- clear
